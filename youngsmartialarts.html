Young's Martial Arts

A redesign of a martial arts school website.

Wireframes

Starting out I wanted to have a good idea as to where I was going with this redesgin. Putting some wireframes together allowed me to show the client my thought processes and get feedback about how the client wanted users to interact with the site. So I sketched some up in Addobe Illustrator.

The Old vs The New

I wanted to bring elements of martial arts and the logo from the school into the design of the new website. The previous one was straightforward and clean, but I wanted to expand upon the idea of a kid centered and family friendly school. A place that would be pleasing to both age groups as they visited and viewed the site. The first thing users see is a scrolling full page header, none of the photos are stock, so you get to see current students. I created the background image you see in the "Our Classes" section in Photoshop. I overlayed the background with a color that stayed within the client's logo scheme via the CMS. Again the background behind the "Our Instructors" section was set up in Adobe Photoshop. Check out the differences between the old website, on the left, and my redesign, on the right!
